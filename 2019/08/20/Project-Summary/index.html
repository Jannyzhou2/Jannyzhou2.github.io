Project Summary

2019-08-20

1.What is my project?

Breast cancer transcriptome and proteome data analysis.

2.What are my research target and research method?

The aim of the research is to use python to analyze breast cancer transcriptome and proteome data.

3.What was the biggest challenge that I encountered? How did I overcome it?

Since I never learnt anything about computer programming before, learning python in the project was the biggest challenge for me. Before the start of the project, I watched videos about python and learnt something like “dictionary” and “string”. At the beginning of the project, since the coding was difficult, I bought a programming book for beginners and spent at least one hour in learning it in the first month instead of giving up. Although I still can’t say I master python, I am sure that I have a deep understanding about python. In addition to the help of books, I also watched some online videos. I independently searched for some problems I didn’t understand in programming and modified them with the help of my teacher. This experience was very meaningful to me, and made me challenge myself and get in touch with a new field that I had never learnt before.

4.What was the most interesting part of my project?

PCA for cell type clustering

Principle Component Analysis(PCA) is a method for compressing a lot of data into something that could capture the essence of original data. What it does is basically taking a dataset of high dimensions and reducing the data to lower dimensions so I can look at it easier. The data analysis is interesting because it uses figure as a way to observe dataset instead of just looking at the data.

5.What have I learned in this project? Anything more I want to add?

Firstly, in this project, I further learnt some biological background information, such as the cause of breast cancer. Breast cancer starts when cells in the breast begin to grow out of control. These cells usually form a tumor that can be seen on an X-ray or felt as a lump. If the cells invade the surrounding tissues or spread to distant areas of the body, the tumor is malignant. Breast cancer is caused by mutations in DNA, but other related risk factors are not yet known exactly how they cause the normal cells to become cancer. So the goal od the project is to analyze breast cancer transcriptome and proteome data using python. (transcriptome data set: RNA-seq can facilitate the ability to look at difference in gene expression in different groups; proteome data set: proteomics can confirm the presence of the protein and provides a direct measure of the quantity present.)

Secondly, I also learnt some data analysis using python. For example, I learnt PCA plot for RNA data and proteomics data, Differential Gene Expression(DGE) Analysis for RNA-seq profiling and RNA-seq data analysis pipeline. Because the mutated cells behave differently than the normal cells, we need to know what genetic mechanism is causing the difference, which means we need to look at differences in gene expression. As a result, we can use RNA-seq to measure gene expression in normal cells and mutated cells and compare the two cell types.

Because the project mainly focuses on computer programing, I hope that there will be more background information about breast cancer next time.
